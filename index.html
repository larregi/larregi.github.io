About me

User centered approach and scalable design files is my way to go

I was born in the beautiful coast city of San Sebastián, on the northern coast of Spain. The place where I did most of my studies and where I grew a deep love for gastronomy, nature, and Basque culture.

I'm a user-centred focus designer with a large experience in different projects and companies. My design process involves user research, analysis of data and usability testing, and collaborating with multi discipline teams in agile and iterative processes and methodologies.

My practice leans on research, data, and iteration. I've worked with startups and established companies, shaping products that are both intuitive and scalable.

I can highlight my mobile design experience as I have been taking part in different projects involving both Hybrid and Native applications. I'm also experienced working with web platforms and having direct communication with Front-End developers and QA engineers, leading the design decisions and process.

Outside of work I climb, hike, and shoot photo and video, hobbies that bring me a healthy balance between digital world and nature.
